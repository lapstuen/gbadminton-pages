Privacy Policy for gBadminton

Last updated: January 27, 2026

1. Introduction

gBadminton ("the App") is a private badminton club management application developed by 1955.no. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

The App collects and stores the following information:

  • Name: Your display name for session registration
  • Wallet Balance: Your current account balance in Thai Baht (THB)
  • Session Registrations: Record of which badminton sessions you have registered for
  • Transaction History: History of payments and balance adjustments
  • Device Token: For push notifications about new sessions

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for:

  • Managing badminton session registrations
  • Tracking wallet balance and payments
  • Sending push notifications about new sessions or announcements
  • Displaying player lists during active sessions

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely in Google Firebase Cloud Firestore. We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Password-protected user accounts
  • Secure Firebase authentication and access rules
  • Regular security updates

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law. Your data is only visible to:

  • You (your own account information)
  • Club administrators (for session management purposes)
  • Other players (only your name and registration status during active sessions)

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Cloud database and push notifications (Firebase Privacy Policy)

7. Push Notifications

With your permission, we send push notifications to inform you about:

  • New badminton sessions available for registration
  • Important announcements from club administrators

You can disable push notifications at any time in your device settings.

8. Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as you are an active member of the badminton club. If you wish to delete your account, please contact the club administrator.

9. Children's Privacy

This App is intended for club members aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the App
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Opt out of push notifications

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact:
