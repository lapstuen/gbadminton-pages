How do I register for a session?

1. Open the app and login
2. Wait for a new session to be published by the administrator
3. Tap the green "Register" button
4. Your balance will be automatically deducted

How do I check my balance?

Your current balance is displayed at the top of the main screen. Tap "View Transactions" to see your complete transaction history.

Can I leave a session after registering?

Yes! Tap the red "Leave" button before the session is locked. Your payment will be refunded to your wallet automatically.

What if my balance is low?

Contact the club administrator to top up your wallet. You cannot register for sessions if your balance is insufficient.

I'm not receiving push notifications

1. Go to iPhone Settings ‚Üí gBadminton
2. Tap "Notifications"
3. Enable "Allow Notifications"
4. Make sure notifications are not set to "Do Not Disturb"

I forgot my password

Contact the club administrator to reset your password. For security reasons, password reset is handled manually.

Can I use the app on multiple devices?

Yes! You can login with the same account on multiple devices (iPhone, iPad). Your balance and session registrations will sync automatically.

What are the color indicators?

Green: Good balance (can play 3+ sessions)
Orange: Medium balance (1-2 sessions left)
Red: Low balance (less than 1 session)
